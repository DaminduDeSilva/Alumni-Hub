## üë• How to Use Alumni Hub

### **For Alumni (New Users)**

#### 1. **Register with Google**
- Visit the website
- Click **"Continue with Google"**
- Sign in with your Google account
- You'll be automatically registered

#### 2. **Submit Your Information**
- After login, click **"Submit Alumni Data"**
- Fill out your profile:
  - Personal details (name, email, phone)
  - Academic information (batch, field of study)
  - Professional details (current job, company)
  - Upload your photo
- Click **"Submit Application"**

#### 3. **Wait for Approval**
- Your status will show **"Pending Verification"**
- A field administrator will review your application
- You'll see **"Verified"** once approved

#### 4. **Access Alumni Directory**
- Once verified, browse the **"Alumni Directory"**
- Find batchmates by engineering field
- View contact information of other verified alumni

---

### **For Field Administrators**

#### 1. **Login**
- Use the **"Committee member? Admin Login"** link
- Enter your email and password
- Access your field-specific dashboard

#### 2. **Review Applications**
- See pending applications for your engineering field
- Click **"Review"** on any submission
- View detailed applicant information in popup
- **Approve** or **Reject** with reasons

#### 3. **Manage Your Field**
- View statistics for your field
- Track approval/rejection counts
- Monitor recent activities

---

### **For Super Administrators**

#### 1. **Full System Access**
- Login: `admin@alumni.edu` / `Admin@123`
- Access complete admin dashboard

#### 2. **Assign Field Admins**
- Go to **"Field Admins"** section
- Assign administrators to specific engineering fields
- Remove field admin access when needed

#### 3. **Oversee All Applications**
- Review submissions from all fields
- View system-wide statistics
- Manage user roles and permissions

## ‚ùì Common Questions

**Q: I submitted my data but status shows "Pending"**  
A: Your application is being reviewed. You'll be notified once approved.

**Q: I can't access the alumni directory**  
A: Only verified alumni can access the directory. Wait for admin approval.

**Q: How do I become a field administrator?**  
A: Contact the super administrator to request field admin access.

**Q: Can I update my information after approval?**  
A: Yes, verified users can update their profiles anytime.
